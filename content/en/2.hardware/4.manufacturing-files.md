## Assembly Notes

The board is clearly designed for PCBA-friendly manufacturing:

- dense passive usage in `0402`, `0603`, and `0805`
- QFN, TQFN, VSSOP, and LGA packages
- FPC camera and OLED connectors
- board-edge external connectors for battery, speaker, motor, button, and microphone

## Fabrication Workflow

1. Review the schematic and board PDFs.
2. Upload gerbers from `keero-hardware/mainboard/fabrication/gerbers`.
3. Use the BOM and pick-place files for PCBA.
4. Validate orientation and connector population against the board render images.
5. Use the STEP and STL files to verify fit before enclosure production.
