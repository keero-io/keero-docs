# System Architecture

The current Keero mainboard is organized around six practical blocks.

## 1. Compute

- `U2`: `ESP32-S3-WROOM-1U-N16R8`
- Native USB data is routed as `USB_D_P` and `USB_D_N`
- Multiple GPIOs are routed to camera, OLED, pogo, haptic, and sensor interfaces
- The board explicitly calls out strapping pins `IO0`, `IO3`, `IO45`, and `IO46`
- The schematic also notes non-usable pins `IO35`, `IO36`, and `IO37`

## 2. Power Management

- `U1`: `AXP2101`
- Battery connector: `CN1`
- USB-C connector: `USB1`
- Charging indicator LED: `LED1`
- Generated rails visible in schematic labels:
  - `3V3A`
  - `3V3B`
  - `2V8A`
  - `DV_2.8V`
  - `1V5A`
  - `VPP` and `VPPA`
- `SW1` is the `PWRON` switch routed into the PMIC

## 3. Vision and Display

- Camera connector: `CN2` for `OV2640`
- OLED connector: `CN5`
- Display power is generated with the `SX1308` boost section
- Important signal labels visible in the schematic:
  - `CSI_D0` to `CSI_D7`
  - `CSI_HSYNC`
  - `CSI_VSYNC`
  - `CSI_PCLK`
  - `CSI_MCLK`
  - `CAM_RST`
  - `CAM_PWDN`
  - `DISP_CS`
  - `DISP_RST`
  - `DISP_SCK`
  - `DISP_MOSI`

The schematic includes a design note that `CAM_RST` and `CAM_PWDN` are held in their normal state because no additional controllable GPIOs were available.

## 4. Audio and Haptics

- `U5`: `ICS-41350` digital microphone
- `U3`: `MAX98357A` I2S amplifier
- `CN3`: speaker connector
- `U6`: `DRV2605L` haptic driver
- `CN4`: motor connector for the haptic actuator

Relevant audio nets seen in the schematic:

- `BCLK`
- `LCLK`
- `DIN`
- `SD`
- `BCLK1`
- `DIN1`

## 5. Motion and User Input

- `U4`: `LIS2DW12TR` accelerometer
- `SW4`: user button on the board
- `H2` and `H5`: user button board/header connection points
- `ACC_INT` and `ACC_INTA` are exposed as interrupt signals

## 6. Expansion and Docking

- `J1`: pogo connector
- `H1`: bottom header
- `H4`: ESP breakout header
- The board exposes UART, I2C, power, and IRQ-related nets to external hardware

Signals visible in the schematic for interconnect and expansion include:

- `TXD`, `RXD`, `TXDA`, `RXDA`
- `I2C_SCL`, `I2C_SDA`, `I2C_SCLA`, `I2C_SDAA`
- `PWR_IRQ`, `PWR_IRQA`
- `USER_BUTTON_BOARD`
- `MIC_BOARD`
